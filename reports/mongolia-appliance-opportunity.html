Go-To-Market Moves

Channel

  • Unitel push: SMS + in-app banners for 700k linked homes
  • TOKI spotlight: category landing with seasonal badges
  • Influencer demos for ovens and compact washers

Offers

  • Bundles: fridge + surge protector; washer + detergent plan
  • Financing: 3/6/12 month BNPL with instant eligibility via Unitel data
  • Installation vouchers embedded in checkout

Operations

  • Stock for winter: prioritize energy-efficient SKUs
  • Service SLA: 48h delivery + install in Ulaanbaatar core
  • Returns: clear 7-day DOA/defect path in-app

Measurement

  • Track: category CVR, attach rates, repeat within 90 days
  • A/B: price anchoring and bundle prominence
  • Heatmaps: scroll-to-cart on product pages
